    Welcome

    This will be the official U.S. government website for people who make, receive, and manage federal awards.

    What Can I Do Here?

    Federal Assistance

    Assistance Listings (CFDA)

    This website has officially replaced CFDA.gov.
    • Learn More
    • Search Assistance Listings

    Federal users should create a user account, migrate their roles from CFDA, and officially manage their listings here.

    Entity and public users may search for assistance listings, create a user account, and save searches.

    Contracting

    FBO.gov (opens in new window),  FPDS.gov (opens in new window),  and  WDOL.gov (opens in new window)  remain the authoritative sources for contract information. To prepare for the future, we encourage you to test search, user accounts, and saved searches here and provide  feedback.
